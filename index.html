A proper local for pints, supper, and a dining room worth booking ahead.

The Lantern Arms serves seasonal pub food, local ales, and a short thoughtful wine list in a restored riverside building that works just as well for Tuesday supper as it does for Sunday lunch or a full-table celebration.

What people come for

Good rooms, good timing, and food that earns repeat visits

The Lantern Arms is known for fish pie on cold evenings, a disciplined burger, hand-pulled local ale, and a Sunday dining room that fills quickly enough to justify booking without turning the place stiff.

House fixtures

  • Quiz night every Tuesday from 8.00pm.
  • Thursday pie and pint offer in the front bar.
  • Sunday roast until sold out.
  • Private dining room for up to 18 guests.
