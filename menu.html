Kitchen and bar

Seasonal pub classics, well-kept ale, and a short wine list that behaves itself.

The menu changes with the week, but the promise stays the same: properly made pub food, generous portions, and a back bar that is as useful for a quick pint as it is for a longer evening.

From the kitchen

Plates that keep the room full

Beer-battered haddock, steak and stout pie, roast chicken schnitzel, cider-braised pork, and a moving market fish special lead the supper board through most of the year.

From the bar

Local pints and a sensible glass list

Three rotating local ales, one guest lager, a short but well-bought wine list, and a house cocktail menu built for early evenings rather than nightclub theatrics.
